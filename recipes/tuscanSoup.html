Tuscan Soup

Description

Tuscan soup with spicy sausages, potatoes, and spinach.

Ingredients

  • Spicy Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Chicken broth
  • Potatoes
  • Fresh spinach
  • Milk
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place sausage and onion into a large stockpot over medium heat. Break sausage up with a spoon and cook until onion is translucent and sausage is browned and crumbly, 7 to 8 minutes. Drain if desired.
  2. Add chicken broth and potatoes; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until potatoes are tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir in evaporated milk. Taste and season with salt and pepper as needed.
